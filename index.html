About De Friends Catering

Based in Bugembe, Jinja City, we are passionate about creating unforgettable culinary experiences for all your special events.

Founded with a passion for exceptional food and service, De Friends Catering has become a trusted name for events throughout Jinja City and beyond. We specialize in creating memorable dining experiences for weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and all special occasions.

Our team combines traditional Ugandan flavors with modern culinary techniques to deliver dishes that delight the senses. From local specialties to international cuisine, we craft menus that reflect your unique taste and vision.
